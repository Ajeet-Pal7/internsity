About Us

Your experience is everything to us

“The future belongs to those who prepare for it today.”
– Malcolm X

Welcome to Internsity, a pioneering platform designed to revolutionize the way students experience internships. We aim to create a bridge between academic learning and real-world industry exposure, ensuring that every student not only learns but thrives in a collaborative and innovative environment.

Who we are?

Internsity is a forward-thinking organization founded with the belief that internships should be more than a checkbox for resumes—they should be transformative experiences that prepare students for the demands of the professional world. We are a team of tech enthusiasts, educators, and industry professionals united by a common goal: to nurture the next generation of IT leaders through hands-on learning, mentorship, and innovative project development.

What we do?

At Internsity, we take students on a structured internship journey that mirrors the real-world hiring and project management processes. Through rigorous selection, interdisciplinary collaboration, and mentorship, we empower students to work on live projects, gaining practical knowledge and experience.

  • Rigourous Selection Process
    Students undergo a CIT (Common Internship Test) to evaluate their technical and analytical skills.
    Shortlisted candidates face a Technical Interview to test problem-solving and coding expertise.
    The final round, the HR Interview, assesses communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Team Formation
    Selected students are grouped into interdisciplinary teams consisting of UI/UX designers, system analysts, and developers. Each team is led by a designated Team Leader.

  • Project Allocation
    Teams are assigned challenging projects to develop solutions collaboratively.

  • Mentorship and Guidance
    Each team is paired with a mentor who tracks their progress, provides insights, and resolves challenges.

  • Final Submission and Presentation
    Teams present their completed projects, demonstrating their work and learning.

  • Certificate Allocation
    Upon successful completion, students receive certificates that remain live on our platform for future verification.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical knowledge, foster their technical skills, and prepare them to tackle real-world challenges with confidence and creativity. We aim to create a platform where students transform into industry-ready professionals through hands-on experience and mentorship.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every IT student has access to meaningful learning opportunities, bridging the gap between education and industry. Our goal is to set a new standard for internships by combining technology, mentorship, and innovation to redefine the professional journey of students.
